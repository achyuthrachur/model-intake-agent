Implementation and Controls Memo - Horizon Credit Scorecard v3.2

1. Deployment Architecture
Model is deployed as a versioned service within the credit decisioning platform.
Inputs are validated against schema rules before scoring.
Score outputs and reason codes are persisted for downstream reporting.

2. Access and Security
Production changes require ticket approval and separation of duties.
Write access is limited to authorized engineering and MRM administrators.
Service secrets are managed through centralized key management.

3. Change Management
All material changes require model owner approval and MRM notification.
Release artifacts include test evidence, rollback plan, and control sign-off.
Emergency changes require post-implementation review within five business days.

4. Monitoring and Reporting
Monthly monitoring includes drift metrics, override rates, and realized performance trends.
Quarterly governance package includes incidents, enhancements, and remediation status.

5. Contingency
Fallback process routes affected applications to manual underwriting if service degradation occurs.
Business continuity runbooks are reviewed semi-annually.
